Kant's critique of pure reason remains highly relevant today, especially as we develop artificial intelligence that mimics human reasoning.

<Blockquote variant="info">
  "All our knowledge begins with the senses, proceeds then to the understanding, and ends with reason. There is nothing higher than reason." — Immanuel Kant
</Blockquote>

## The Boundaries of AI

While AI can process vast amounts of data and identify complex patterns, it lacks the transcendental apperception that Kant argued is essential for true understanding. AI operates within the realm of phenomena, but it cannot grasp the noumenal world—the things-in-themselves.
