The original social contract, as envisioned by Rousseau and Hobbes, relied on a centralized authority (the state) to enforce rules and protect citizens. Today, digital decentralization technologies like blockchain are challenging this paradigm.

<Blockquote variant="default">
  "Man is born free, and everywhere he is in chains." — Jean-Jacques Rousseau
</Blockquote>

## Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs)

DAOs represent a new form of social organization where rules are encoded in smart contracts rather than enforced by a central authority. This shift raises profound questions about governance, accountability, and the nature of the social contract in the 21st century.
